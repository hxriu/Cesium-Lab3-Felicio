Skullgirls

The Skullheart, an ancient relic that has the power to grant any wish so long as the wish was pure, has fallen into the hands of Marie, turning her into the Skullgirl. Eight other girls, all of which have their own motivation for getting the Skullheart or killing Marie, are now on the hunt for the Skullgirl.

5 reasons why this game is my favorite:

  • The game is fun and engaging.
  • The character designs are cool.
  • The main story is interesting, and shows each characters' backgrounds.
  • The gacha in the game is addicting.
  • The skins, moves, and cutscenes are well-animated.
